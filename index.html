About

Our Story

We are your strategic partner

JRA Law Associates LLP is a legal firm established in 2003 by Adv. Ajay Corriea, with a commitment of delivering top-notch legal solutions to clients within the Mumbai Metropolitan Region and mainly around Palghar and Thane Districts. The firm has 2 partners and around 15 associates working on litigation and non-litigation projects. Utilising the expertise of our team of highly experienced attorneys, our firm has established a distinguished track record of providing outstanding legal counsel and customised strategies to assist our clients in embarking upon solutions which are best suited and in a timely manner.

Practice Areas

Property Law

Unraveling the intricacies of property transactions, our Property Law team facilitates seamless real estate deals with precision, offering comprehensive legal support from acquisition to title verification.

Corporate Law

Elevating your business strategies, our Corporate Law practice tailors legal solutions to safeguard your enterprise, offering insightful counsel on corporate governance, compliance, and contractual matters for sustained success.

Criminal Law

Defending your rights with vigor, our Criminal Law practitioners bring a wealth of experience to the courtroom, tirelessly advocating for clients facing criminal charges and striving for optimal outcomes.

Family Law

Navigating sensitive family matters, our Family Law experts provide compassionate and strategic counsel, ensuring resolutions that prioritize the well-being of all involved parties during challenging times.

Consumer Protection Law

Empowering consumers with legal advocacy, our Consumer Protection Law division safeguards your rights, offering redress and guidance in cases of unfair practices, product liability, and consumer rights violations.

Labour Law

Providing expert guidance in navigating the complexities of employment relationships, our Labour Law practice ensures businesses and employees alike benefit from fair and legally sound workplace solutions.
